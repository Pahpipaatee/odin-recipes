Jalapeño Cheese Rolls

Description

Jalapeño Cheese Rolls are soft, buttery rolls filled with a generous amount of spicy chopped jalapeño peppers and melted cheddar cheese

creating a delightful combination of savory and heat in each bite; perfect as a snack, appetizer, or side dish with a kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons yeast
  • 2 cups finely shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 4 ounces pepperoni, chopped (optional)
  • 3 jalapeños, thinly sliced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a bread machine pan, place milk, 2 eggs, butter, sugar, salt, bread flour, and yeast in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Run dough cycle. Remove dough from the machine after the cycle is done, about 90 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place dough out on a lightly floured surface. Roll out into a 1/4-inch thick rectangle about 16x20 inches.
  4. Sprinkle 3/4 cup cheese on dough, all the way to the edges. Add pepperoni and jalapeños. Finally, sprinkle with another 3/4 cup cheese.
  5. With the long side of the dough rectangle facing you, roll dough tightly into a log; slice into 1-inch-thick rounds.
  6. Place rounds 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet.
  7. Whisk remaining egg with 1 tablespoon water in a small bowl. Brush rolls with egg wash. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup cheese on top of rolls.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 8 to 12 minutes.
